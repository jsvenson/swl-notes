## Tier 1/3

Sequence One

    5 25 29 85 89 ??

Answer: 145

Reason: take the sum of the squares of the previous term. So:

    5^2 = 25
    2^2 + 5^2 = 4 + 24 = 29
    2^2 + 9^2 = 4 + 81 = 85
    8^2 + 5^2 = 64 + 25 = 89
    8^2 + 9^2 = 64 + 81 = 145

    1 + 2 = 3
    3 + 3 = 6

    15 + 6 = 21

    28 + 8 = 36
