## Tier 1/3

Sequence One

    5 25 29 85 89 ??

Answer: 145

Reason: take the sum of the squares of the previous term. So:

    5^2 = 25
    2^2 + 5^2 = 4 + 25 = 29
    2^2 + 9^2 = 4 + 81 = 85
    8^2 + 5^2 = 64 + 25 = 89
    8^2 + 9^2 = 64 + 81 = 145

    1 3 6 10 15 21 28 36 45

Answer: 55

Reason: add sequential integers to current value to get new value:

    1 + 2 = 3
    3 + 3 = 6
    6 + 4 = 10
    10 + 5 = 15
    15 + 6 = 21
    21 + 7 = 28
    28 + 8 = 36
    36 + 9 = 45
    45 + 10 = 55

Sequence Two

    2 3 3 5 10 13 39 43 172 177

Answer: 885

Reason: add 1, multiply by 1, add 2, multiply by 2 ... :

    2 + 1 = 3
    3 * 1 = 3
    3 + 2 = 5
    5 * 2 = 10
    10 + 3 = 13
    13 * 3 = 39
    39 + 4 = 43
    43 * 4 = 172
    172 + 5 = 177
    177 * 5 = 885
